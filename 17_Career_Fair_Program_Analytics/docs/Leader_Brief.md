# Leader Brief

## Decision summary

The 2023 career fair appears to have delivered a positive employer and student experience, but the strongest improvement opportunity is not atmosphere or operations. It is turnout quality: more prepared students and stronger candidate volume for employers.

## What is working

- Students rated employer approachability, layout, and overall value positively.
- Employers rated the atmosphere and registration process positively.
- Event organization appears solid enough to support incremental improvements rather than a full redesign.

## Where to act next

- Increase candidate volume with targeted promotion through faculty announcements, email, and other high-reach channels.
- Improve candidate readiness with a short pre-event preparation module and earlier CCC touchpoints.
- Improve ergonomics and accessibility for employers, especially around table layout and long-duration standing.

## Next-cycle success metrics

- Student check-ins
- Employer satisfaction on candidate volume and candidate quality
- Student self-report of preparedness
- Application intent and post-event follow-up rates
